DIY Compatibility Readiness Checklist

Before downloading or testing a Nonaym DIY package, use this static checklist to decide whether your target device is likely to be a good fit. This checklist does not submit information, create an account, or start an installation.

  • Device type: a 64-bit x86_64 computer is the primary DIY target for common PC-style installs.
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM minimum; 8 GB or more recommended.
  • Storage: 32 GB minimum; 64 GB SSD or larger recommended.
  • Network: wired Ethernet is strongly recommended for setup and reliable DNS service.
  • Boot support: the device should support booting from a USB installer.
  • Data safety: the target computer may be erased during installation. Back up anything important first.
  • Beginner caution: do not test on your only computer unless you are comfortable reinstalling it.

Recommended Setup

To ensure optimal performance and compatibility, we recommend the following:

  • 8 GB RAM minimum
  • 64 GB SSD or larger
  • Wired Ethernet connection
  • Reliable power supply

How to Prepare

We recommend that you:

  1. Check your computer's specifications against our requirements
  2. Ensure you have a USB drive ready for installation
  3. Backup any important data from the target computer
